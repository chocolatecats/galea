Shifting the Narrative on Sharks and the Sea

Galea Marine Collective is a creative studio dedicated to reshaping the way we see ocean life, with a special focus on sharks. We use design to shift how society views marine creatures and advocate for their conservation.

FEATURED WORK

A richly illustrated book that uses data visualisation, typography, and art to reframe how society views sharks.

PURCHASE THE BOOK

Sharks are widely misunderstood. While often perceived as ruthless and monstrous, the majority are shy and harmless.

Sharks play a vital role in keeping marine ecosystems healthy and in balance.

Overfishing, bycatch, habitat loss, and fear-driven media have led to a 71% global decline in shark populations.

Reframing people’s perceptions of sharks is essential in order to advocate for their conservation.

Taking care of sharks means taking care of the ocean. And taking care of the ocean means taking care of the planet.


ABOUT

I’m Juliette — a designer and illustrator from Sydney, Australia, and a big fan of sharks. This project began as part of my university honours thesis, where I explored how visual communication can help reshape the way we see sharks. The more I researched, the more I realised how misunderstood sharks are, and how powerful visual storytelling has the power to shift fear into curiosity. Galea Marine Collective was born from a love for the ocean and a belief that design can make a difference.
